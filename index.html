How we work

Spot. Build. Run. Report.

One short loop. Hands-on through every step, with you in the seat.

01 · SPOT

30-min audit

We sit with you and pull up your phone logs, inbox, and calendar. Together we find what's leaking — calls missed at 6pm, quotes ghosting, photos lost in texts.

02 · BUILD

One worker, one bottleneck

Our team designs the right AI employee for the leak. Trained on your services, your prices, the way your crew actually talks. No template.

03 · RUN

Installed live

Goes live alongside your team. You see every reply. Anger, ambiguity, dollars over a threshold — escalates to a human on our side and yours.

04 · REPORT

Friday memo

One page in your inbox each Friday: dollars saved, hours reclaimed, what's stuck, what to automate next. No graphs. Just numbers.

Where humans stay involved

No black-box automation. Ever.

Three commitments that make this an operations company, not a chatbot vendor.

01

Approval gates

Every escalation routes to a human — anger, ambiguity, dollar amounts over a threshold you set. AI handles the loop; you handle judgment.

02

Live receipts

Every reply, file, status, and approval is visible. We don't hide anything in a model. You can read every line that goes out under your name.

03

Local accountability

The Wilmington team installs, monitors, and tunes — by name. If something breaks, you're texting a person, not a help-desk queue.

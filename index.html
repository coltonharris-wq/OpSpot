How it works

Spot → Build → Run → Report.

One short loop. You stay in it.

01 · Spot

30-min audit

I pull up your phone logs, inbox, and calendar with you. We find what's leaking — calls missed at 6pm, quotes ghosting, photos lost in texts.

02 · Build

One worker, one bottleneck

I design the right AI employee for the leak. Trained on your services, your prices, the way your team actually talks. No template.

03 · Run

Installed live

Goes live alongside your team. You see every reply. Anger, ambiguity, dollars over a threshold — straight to your phone.

04 · Report

Friday memo

One page in your inbox each Friday: dollars saved, hours reclaimed, what's stuck, what to automate next.
